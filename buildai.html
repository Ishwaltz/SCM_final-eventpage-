About the Event

Join us for an immersive 4-day hybrid event exploring the cutting edge of artificial intelligence. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, "Build with AI" offers something for everyone.

Through hands-on workshops, enlightening talks, and collaborative sessions, you'll gain practical knowledge about Machine Learning, Generative AI, Natural Language Processing, and AI-driven applications.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the registration fee?

This event is completely free for all participants. However, registration is mandatory as seats are limited.

Do I need prior knowledge of AI or programming?

Basic programming knowledge is recommended, but not required. We have sessions designed for beginners as well as advanced learners.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes, all participants who attend at least 3 out of 4 days will receive a digital certificate of participation.

What should I bring to the event?

For in-person attendees, please bring your laptop with a stable internet connection. Online participants should ensure they have a good internet connection and a Zoom account.

Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes, all sessions will be recorded and made available to registered participants for a limited time after the event.
